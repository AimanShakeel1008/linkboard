LinkBoard · System Design, Built From Scratch

Course Hub & Roadmap

One place to see the whole journey: every chapter and lesson, where you are right now, and what comes next. We build a real Hacker News–style link platform one layer at a time — from raw sockets up to a scaled, observable, cloud-run system. Start at the top and work down.

How to use this page

The roadmap shows the eight major stages of the course. Below it, every phase is listed with a status badge: Done lessons are finished and clickable, Now is the one we're building, and Planned phases are on the map but not written yet. This page is updated whenever a lesson is added or completed.

1. The roadmap — you are here

The course is a straight climb through eight stages. Each stage groups several phases that share a theme. You are at the very start of the climb, in Bedrock.

2. Legend

  • Done — the lesson is written and the project runs end-to-end at that phase. The row links straight to it.
  • Now — the phase we're actively building. Also clickable.
  • Planned — on the roadmap, not written yet. Shown greyed and not clickable.
